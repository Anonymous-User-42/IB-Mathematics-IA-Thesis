

\subsection{{Trigonometric Series}}
	
	{A trigonometric series is a series that is of the form,}
		
		$$\sum_{n = 0}^{\infty}\left(a_n\cdot\cos\left(nx\right) + b_n\cdot\sin\left(nx\right)\right)$$
		
	{Where, $a_n$ and $b_n$ are some arbitrary functions or constants.}
	
	{When $a_n$ and $b_n$ are of the form below, they are known as the \textbf{Fourier series}.}
		
		$$a_n = \frac{1}{L}\cdot\int_L{f(x)\cdot\cos\left(nx\right)dx$$
		
		$$a_n = \frac{1}{L}\cdot\int_L{f(x)\cdot\sin\left(nx\right)dx$$
